House Prices across Australia

Houses were the most sold house property type between September 2018 and June 2020. This was then followed by units and then townhouses. The most properties were sold in Victoria and New South Wales which makes these areas the hottest for property!

How much will more Bedrooms Cost?

We can see that the average property price increases with the number of bedrooms a property has. The average prices sharply increase after four bedrooms meaning perhaps it is not worth getting a fifth bedroom!

Location, Location, Location!

For every city the most expensive properties are in the city regions. In general, the further away you area from the city centre the more the property prices will fall.
